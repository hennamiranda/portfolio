methods

"Beauty and brains, pleasure and usability - They should go hand in hand." - Don Norman

This is the objective towards which I work with these methods.

design thinking

Solving problems and discovering new opportunities with a creative and a solution-based mindset through empathizing, brainstorming, prototyping and iteration.

lean UX

Uniting product development and business through Lean Startup ’learning-loops’ of build-measure-learn . Embracing the agile design processes to improve effectiveness.

user-centered design

The human is the core of the design. To create usable, desirable and accessable products, we must solve real problems of real users. This is how we create a positive impact.

about me

I am a UX/UI Designer with a passion for growth and a love for simplicity.

My goal is to enhance the business while having happier users. For me it is essential that the design fulfils both the business requirements and the customers’ needs.

I am driven by authenticity, curiosity and desire to learn. And I am also a very human driven person who enjoys teamwork and brainstorming in order to find the best solutions.

My working languages are English, Spanish and Finnish. Oh yes…I am from Finland!
